## ğŸ’¡ Indian Cooking Tips

### Healthy Cooking Methods:
1. **Less Oil:**
   - Use 1-2 tbsp for dal
   - 2-3 tbsp for sabzi
   - Avoid deep frying

2. **Tadka:**
   - Use mustard oil or ghee (limited)

3. **Grilling/Roasting:**
   - Tandoori chicken (no oil)
   - Roasted vegetables

4. **Steaming:**
   - Idli, dhokla
   - Steamed vegetables

5. **Boiling:**
   - Dal, rajma, chole
   - Eggs, chicken
